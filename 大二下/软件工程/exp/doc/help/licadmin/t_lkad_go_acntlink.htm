Accessing AccountLink on the Web

You can access AccountLink through the IBM Web site.

To access AccountLink:
  1. Go to the IBM support for Rational products Licensing page on the Web.
    • In the LKAD, click License Keys > Get, Return, or Move Keys.
    • In a Web browser, go to http://www.ibm.com/software/rational/support/licensing/.
  2. Click the IBM Rational Licensing and Download Center link and logon using a current IBM ID and password. If you do not have an IBM ID, click Register to create a new one.
  3. At the page for Rational Download and Licensing Center, click the hyperlink at Request and Manage License Keys.
  4. At the page for IBM Rational Software License Support, click on the hyperlink for AccountLink.
