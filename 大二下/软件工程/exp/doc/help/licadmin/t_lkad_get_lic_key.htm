Getting license keys

You can use the AccountLink online licensing tool to get your IBM Rational license keys.

Note: AccountLink will require some information from you to complete your transaction. Before you start, you should know:
  • Your account number
  • Your product name
  • Your product's license type
These are found on your Proof of Entitlement sent or e-mailed to you when you purchased your software.
To get your IBM Rational license keys:
  1. Go to AccountLink.
  2. Click the Get license key(s) link.
  3. Complete the steps to provide your account number, contact e-mail address, and other information.
  4. Receive the license key file that is generated and forwarded to the e-mail address you specified on the AccountLink License Contact page.
Now, depending on the license type, you can do any of the following:
  • Import the floating or named-user floating license key file on the license server.
  • Import the node-locked license key file on the desktop.
  • Finding the hostname and host ID of a computer
    The hostname and host ID are required information in AccountLink.
